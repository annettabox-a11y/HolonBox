HOLON: Responsible AI for Org Health

A 0→1 diagnostic that turns team friction into frameworks in under 2 minutes. Built with Claude + context engineering. No raw LLM advice.

0→1 Product Responsible AI Org Design Claude + Meta AI

Problem

COOs and CHROs know their teams are burning out, but existing diagnostics take 6 weeks, cost $50k, and produce 40-page decks nobody reads. Meanwhile, execs are scared to use ChatGPT for org advice because it hallucinates and lacks citations.

Research insight: I interviewed 15 COOs/CHROs. 100% said “I’d pay for a tool that gives me a diagnosis in 2 min if I could trust the output and show my board the logic.”

PRD Constraint

Goal: Deliver a trusted org diagnosis in <2 minutes with 85% completion rate.

Key constraints:

  • No raw LLM advice: All outputs must be mapped to 4 cited frameworks: Systems Thinking, Org Design, Team Topologies, Flow.
  • Human-in-the-loop: User reviews each framework summary before PDF export.
  • Privacy: No user data stored. Session-only.

How It Works: Architecture

Context engineering injects your org’s signals into Claude with the 4 frameworks. Claude returns structured analysis, not free-text advice. This prevents hallucination and gives you citations you can defend to your board.

Responsible AI Checklist

Risk Mitigation
Hallucinated org advice Claude constrained to 4 cited frameworks. No open-ended generation.
Bias in diagnosis Multiple frameworks surface conflicting lenses. Human reviews before export.
Data privacy Zero storage. All processing client-side. PDF generated in browser.
Over-reliance UI labels output as “Diagnostic draft, not prescription.” Requires human sign-off.
